Bolognaise

Description

This recipe involves the use of spaghetti noodles, mince and a whole list of other interesting but delicious ingredients

Ingredients

  • Mince
  • onion
  • Oil
  • tomato paste

    • Steps

    1. put oil and mince in a pan and cook till mince is browned
    2. add chopped onion to mince and simmer
    3. add in tomato paste along with any other spices
    4. cook pasta until el dente and serve with mince overtop
